         *Widths of integer types
         byte is 8 bits (-128 to 127) and can store 246 numbers
         short is 16 bits (-32768 to 32767) and can store a large range of numbers
         int is 32 bits (-2147483648 to 2147483647) and has a much larger range
         long is 64 bits (-9223372036854775808 to 9223372036854775807) and holds an even greater range than the previous
